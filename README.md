# Data Storytelling and Analytical Narrative Workspace

## 1. App name
Graph2

## 2. One-line summary
A public-safe technical case for data storytelling, analytical narrative, and multi-view communication of structured insight.

## 3. Primary user
Analyst or decision communicator

## 4. Secondary user
Operations or product lead

## 5. Problem solved
Analytical outputs often stop at charts, while decision-making needs narrative structure, contextualization, and multiple reading modes.

## 6. Short workflow
Data is prepared, transformed into analytical views, narrated through structured outputs, and shared through public-safe visualization layers.

## 7. Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope covers analytical storytelling patterns, visualization families, and multi-view presentation concepts.

## 8. High-level integrations
High-level only: data preparation, visualization engines, optional retrieval layers, and reporting surfaces.

## 9. What stays private and why
Narrative generation rules, retrieval setup, internal data models, and deployment details remain private because they are part of the operating system.
