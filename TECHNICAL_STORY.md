# Technical Story

## Product framing
Data Storytelling and Analytical Narrative Workspace is documented here as a public-safe technical case. The repository is intended to explain the product shape without exposing product internals.

## Operational or clinical problem
Analytical outputs often stop at charts, while decision-making needs narrative structure, contextualization, and multiple reading modes.

## Product logic
This product is framed as a data storytelling workspace rather than as a generic chart library.

The operational problem is that many analytical systems produce visual output without producing a coherent decision narrative.

The product logic combines data preparation, charting, narrative views, and multiple reading modes for the same analytical material.

The public-safe case keeps the discussion at the level of product patterns, not internal services or data pipelines.

This app is relevant because communication quality is often the difference between analysis that exists and analysis that gets used.

## High-level flow logic
Data is prepared, transformed into analytical views, narrated through structured outputs, and shared through public-safe visualization layers.

## Security boundary
Narrative generation rules, retrieval setup, internal data models, and deployment details remain private because they are part of the operating system.

## Why this app is relevant
This repository matters because it shows a specific product pattern inside the broader thesis that medicine is the asymmetry, data is the method, and web applications are the delivery layer.
